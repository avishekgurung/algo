The rules of games

1.  There can be N number of players who can play this game.
2.  A dice can roll from 1 to 6.
3.  There are 100 areas.
4.  If a player throws 6 then he can throw again.
5.  If a player reaches the start of ladder then he climbs up.
6.  If a player reaches the head of snake, he climbs down.
7.  Any player reaching 100th position first is the winner.

Edge cases:
    Area with snake's head should not be a ladder's end.
    Area with snake's tail should not be a ladder's head.
    If the count goes beyond 100 then the turn goes to another player.
